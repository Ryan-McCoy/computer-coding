About Earth

Earth is a planet in the Sol system that is primarily occupied by humans, as well as many other animals. Humans have many diverse and unique cultures, and many different beliefs. Humans are in what is known as the information age, and are exploring nearby space.



Important things to know about Earth:

  • Civilization on Earth is separated into countries or nations.
  • Each of these nations is comprized of humans, but they still have many cultural differences.
  • These nations often go to war with each other over land and resources.
  • We have enough nuclear weapons to make Earth uninhabitable.
